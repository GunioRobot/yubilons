This form is used only for testing purpose. As long as we don't have your AES key, you cannot use it.
You can also access to the result as follow:

  • TXT output: https://yubico.internux.ch/verify/check/?otp=OTP&h=HASH
We can find your API key with your OTP, and thus check h, which stands for your request signature.
Signature is made as follow: 
args = hmac.new(key, s, digestmod=sha512)
sign = base64.b64encode(args.digest())
where key stands for your API key (ASCII) and s your query (in our case, 'otp=OTP').
